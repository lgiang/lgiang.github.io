About Me

I am a grad student attending California State University in Long Beach, CA. My area of studies is actually in mathematics, I am not doing a thesis, but my favorite areas of math are topology and algebra. Ever since I could remember I knew that I wanted to be a teacher, that changed along the way (because of my fear of being inadequate), but right now I want to teach in community college. I want to get rid of the fear that community college is not good enough, because it is good enough!

Another goal of mine is to help students understand that math shouldn't and isn't scary. I know math can seem scary and foreign, but to me it is like learning a new language, keep on practicing!

I have a small passion for web designing and coding, but I have a terrible habit of being lazy and sometimes being impatient. During my undergrad years I had to take a coding class and learned a bit of Java, MATLAB, and some HTML and CSS (as you can see). Currently I was self-teaching myself Python on CodeAcademy, it is a long and slow process but I do hope that my coding skills will grow.
